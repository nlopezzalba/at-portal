Instructions:

Thank you for taking the time to enroll. This module will ask you questions specific to your Ataxia-Telangiectasia (A-T) diagnosis. The questions below were developed in partnership with the A-T Children’s Project.
Please note this module:
  • Takes approximately 30 minutes to complete.
  • Will refer to the person with the A-T diagnosis as “the participant”.
  • Can be updated at any time by logging in on to the online portal.
  • Once per year, we will ask you to update your module.
If you have any questions while completing this questionnaire, please send an email to support@atfamilies.org or call USA 954.481.6611 and ask to speak with one of the A-T Children’s Project study staff.
