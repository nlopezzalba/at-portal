4. Medical History

This module will ask you questions specific to your ataxia-telangiectasia (A-T) diagnosis.
Please note that this module:
  • Takes approximately 30 minutes to complete.
  • Will refer to the person with the A-T diagnosis as “the participant.”
  • Can be resumed and updated at any time before you submit the survey.
Once per year, we will ask you to update your module.

Note: If you are a parent or guardian of an ataxia-telangiectasia patient, the words “you" and "your” below refer to your child.
