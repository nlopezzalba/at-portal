Research Consent Form - Key Points

“The Global A-T Family Data Platform” initiative is a patient-driven effort overseen by a panel of ataxia-telangiectasia (A-T) advocates that will empower A-T patients and their families to help directly transform research and treatment of the disease by sharing their genetic data and related health information with researchers around the world. If families also agree to allow researchers to re-contact them with follow-up research questions, the platform will enable the A-T community to have a continuous dialog with researchers and potentially accelerate progress in A-T research. The ultimate goal of this effort is to create an environment in which the A-T patient community and their families collect their clinical and genetic data and govern sharing this data with researchers to better understand the disease.

Note: If you are a parent or guardian of an ataxia-telangiectasia patient, the words “you” and “your,” below, refer to your child.
We encourage you to download and read the complete consent form carefully. Please contact us if you have any questions: support@atfamilies.org or USA 954.481.6611.
