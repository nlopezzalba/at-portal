Join Us
How can you participate:
1. Register
Click on “Join Us” to sign up in the system. From there, you will be able to add a member with A-T. The first step for every member is to complete a simple questionnaire with some basic personal data. That information will go through a de-identified process that ensures patient confidentiality.
2. Give us permission
The enrollment process for every member continues with a consent step from where you can give us permission to obtain copies of the member’s medical records and the possibility to collect a saliva sample. We may contact your physician to confirm diagnosis in order to check eligibility.
3. Share your insight and saliva sample
The following step for members is to complete a questionnaire about themselves (current state of the disease, symptoms, etc). We will also send you a simple kit to collect the member saliva sample.
4. Let’s learn together
We will keep you informed about the status of the project and the latest discoveries made possible by your participation. We might also ask additional questions about your experiences to help with future studies.
Together, we have the power to make a real contribution to the health of people with A-T.
